Mulled Apple Juice

Description

Mulled apple juice is a warm, aromatic, non-alcoholic drink. It's made by gently simmering apple juice with mulling spices like cinnamon sticks, cloves, and star anise, often with slices of orange or lemon. This process infuses the juice with a comforting, festive flavor, perfect for chilly days.

Ingredients

To make Mulled Apple Juice,
  • Apple Juice
  • Strips of Orange Peel
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick (plus extra to garnish, if you like)
  • 3 Cloves
  • Sugar (or honey, to taste)

Steps

Here are the simple steps to make Mulled Apple Juice,
  1. Simmer the apple juice with the strips of orange peel, cinnamon stick and cloves for about 5-10 mins until all the flavours have infused. Sweeten to taste.
  2. Serve each drink with a little orange peel and a piece of cinnamon stick, if you like.
